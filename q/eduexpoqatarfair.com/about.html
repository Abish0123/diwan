About Crossway FAQs

Learn more about Crossway Event Management Company and our operations in Qatar and the Middle East.

What is Crossway Event Management Company?
Crossway Event Management Company is a professional event management firm headquartered in Doha, Qatar. We specialize in organizing world-class Expos, Workshops, and Conferences across the Middle East, serving Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Jordan, and Egypt. Crossway works with corporates, government ministries, universities, NGOs, and international organizations to deliver exceptional events throughout the region.
Where is Crossway Event Management Company located?
Crossway Event Management Company is based in Doha, Qatar. Our headquarters in Doha serves as the operational hub for all our event management services across the Middle East. Whether your event is in Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE, or anywhere in the region, the Crossway team manages everything from our Doha base with on-ground support across the Middle East.
Which countries does Crossway Event Management serve?
Crossway Event Management Company provides full event management services across the Middle East, including: Qatar (Headquarters, Doha), Saudi Arabia (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam), UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi), Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman (Muscat), Jordan (Amman), and Egypt (Cairo).
What services does Crossway Event Management Company offer?
Crossway Event Management Company offers three core event management services: Expo & Exhibition Management, Workshop Organization, and Conference & Summit Management. All services are available across Qatar and throughout the Middle East.
